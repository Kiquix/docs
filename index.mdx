---
title: "What is Fred?"
description: "Fred is the user research management platform for UX researchers."
---

Fred helps you design research studies, collect participant responses, and turn raw data into actionable insights — all in one place. Whether you're running a quick 5-second test or a full moderated usability session, Fred gives you the tools to understand your users.

## How Fred works

Fred organizes your research around three building blocks:

<Columns cols={3}>
  <Card title="Projects" icon="folder" href="/projects">
    The top-level container for your research. A project represents a main research goal and can hold multiple studies.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Studies" icon="flask" href="/studies">
    A focused research effort inside a project. Each study contains one or more methods and produces a shareable link for testers.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Methods" icon="chart-bar" href="/methods">
    The tools you use to collect data — card sorting, tree testing, usability tests, surveys, and more.
  </Card>
</Columns>

## What you can do

- **Design studies** using 8 research methods, combining them freely in a single study
- **Share studies** with testers via a simple link — no account required for participants
- **Analyze results** through per-method analytics as responses come in
- **Tag and organize** findings using the Insights hub
- **Build reports** to communicate your research to stakeholders
