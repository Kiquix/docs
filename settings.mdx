The **Settings** page is divided into three sections: **Account**, **Billing**, and **Team**.

<Note>
  Settings is only accessible to the team leader. Invited team members cannot access this page.
</Note>

## Account

Update your personal information:

- Upload a profile picture (avatar)
- Edit your displayed name and surname
- Manage your password
- Connect external storage for study results

To access these options, click **Settings** in the left sidebar, then open the **Account** tab.

## Billing

The **Billing** tab opens the Stripe portal, where you can:

- View your current plan and usage
- Upgrade or downgrade your subscription
- Access billing history and invoices

[Learn more about plans and billing →](/billing/plans)

## Team

The **Team** tab lets you manage who has access to your Fred workspace.

[Manage your team →](/team)
