---
title: "Sharing your study"
description: "How to share a live study with testers."
---

Once a study is live, you can share it with testers using a link. Testers don't need a Fred account to participate.

## Share a study

1. Go to the project containing your study.
2. In the **Studies overview**, find the study you want to share.
3. Click the three-dot menu next to the study.
4. Select **Share link**.
5. Copy the URL from the modal and send it to your testers.

<Warning>
  The **Share link** option is only available for studies that are set to **live**. Draft studies cannot be shared.
</Warning>

## Set a study to live

When you finish building a study, click **Add study to project**. You'll be asked whether to go live immediately or save as a draft. If you saved it as a draft, you can set it to live later from the study settings.
